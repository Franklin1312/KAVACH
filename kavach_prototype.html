The Core Innovation

KAVACH builds a Digital Income Twin (DIT) for each worker — an AI model trained on their personal earning patterns across time, zone, platform, and season. When a disruption triggers, DIT predicts exactly what they would have earned in that window. Payout = 70% of that predicted loss. Not ₹500 flat for everyone.
